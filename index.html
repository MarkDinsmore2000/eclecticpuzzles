Rules and Hints

In each game there is a set of moves provided by a panel of buttons. For each puzzle, observe what the moves available to you are. Then, develop a strategy using the moves available to achieve success. Finally, use your strategy each time you play the game to achieve success. In some of these games, success can be ambiguous Sometimes, you can choose your own goals.

Of course, choosing your own goals is sometimes the hardest part.
