A very Short History

Mr. Newcomb said in my 8th grade Algebra class, that if you don’t feed your mind, your mind will feed on you. I retired due to emotional exhaustion in October of 2024. This project was started six weeks later in order to feed my mind. I’d never done any web development before and hadn’t done any programming for decades and most of that was incidental and in no way professional.

The chances are, given the enormous amount of noise there is on the internet, that at most a handful of people will ever play these games and maybe no one will ever read this. If a few of these games feed a few peoples minds. Please, “accept such portions as apply to” you. Hopefully, the few who find this site will get a few pleasant hours.
